About List Point

Our Mission

List Point exists to make organizing your life simpler and more collaborative. We believe that everyone deserves a straightforward, beautiful tool to create, manage, and share listsâ€”whether you're planning groceries, tracking tasks, or organizing ideas with friends.

What We Do

List Point is a minimalistic web platform designed to help you create and manage lists with ease. From standard to-do lists to dynamic check lists that restore themselves, we offer flexible tools for different needs. You can:

  • Create public or private lists with custom thumbnails and tags
  • Collaborate with others on shared lists
  • Discover and favorite lists from the community
  • Track items with check/uncheck features and undo capabilities
  • Enjoy a clean, modern interface with dark and light themes

Built with Care

List Point is developed and maintained by a solo developer who's passionate about creating software that's both functional and enjoyable to use. Every feature is thoughtfully designed to provide a seamless user experience while keeping things simple and intuitive.

As a one-person operation, I'm committed to continuously improving the platform based on user feedback and evolving needs. Your support helps keep List Point running and growing!
